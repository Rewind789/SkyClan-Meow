Jaywhisker

Jaywhisker is a medicine cat in SkyClan. He is a gray tabby tom with dark blue eyes. He has a pet, a blue butterfly, called Bluewing. He is close friends with Jaywing and Nightheart.

Nightheart

Nightheart is the current leader of SkyClan. He is a black tom with amber eyes, black visor, king cape, and a king crown.
